Filter layer

Range, Pitch Tickets, and Steps decide which C major notes remain in the bowl before the roll.

Shape layer

Bias points toward low, middle, or high notes. Spread decides how wide that area is.

Motion layer

Complexity and Contour matter after the first note because they lean from the previous winning note.
