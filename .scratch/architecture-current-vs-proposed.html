Architecture — Current vs Proposed

Schematic read of the mapped subsystems, to answer one question: overhaul, or targeted changes?

Verdict: targeted changes, not a total overhaul.
The engine core is sound. Every issue is per-feature drift along three axes, each fixable in place:
  1. Note/Curve-era subsystems that never generalized to 8 track types (linking, recording).
  2. Per-feature fan-out — one concept defined/executed in many places (routing 5-sources, engine per-track recompute).
  3. Scattered wall-time (CC rate-limit, modulator dt, slave clock) with no shared service.
No data model needs replacing; no track type needs rewriting. The work is consolidation + two scoped generalizations.
