Teletype

Routing surface is its own world (script-driven CV/trigger in/out, clip/pattern plumbing). Candidates for matrix routing: ClockDivisor / ClockMultiplier / TimeBase. Everything else (CvIn/Out source/dest/range/quantize, scripts, clips, pattern slots) is Teletype-internal. Decide whether Teletype is in the matrix at all, or stays script-routed.

How to use this: the now rows are the existing surface (parity baseline). The cand rows are the keep/drop decisions — that's the musical triage only you can make. structural lines are what I excluded as non-musical; promote any I got wrong. Two known defects are flagged: Stochastic Feel (dead slot) and Stoch/PF Scale/RootNote (base-write). Ranges marked "—" I'll pull once the keep set is settled.
