NAME CROSS CONDUCTOR
CROSS CONDUCTOR - xformer cross-track piece. This TT2 track conducts other
Performer tracks instead of making sound itself.
Layout it expects: track 2 = any pattern track, track 3 = a Teletype track,
track 4 = a Note track, route 1 = some CV source (RT readback).
  I  : reset the section counter, set a half-second metro.
  M  : A counts. Every 8 ticks cycle track 2 across patterns 1..4 (WP.SET).
       Every 4 ticks fire script 1 on track 3 (WS). CV 1 tracks route 1 (RT).
       30% chance: drop a random note on a random step of track 4 (WNN).
  1  : IN 1 fires track 3 script 1 by hand and pulses TR 1.
  2  : IN 2 throws track 2 onto a random pattern (1..4).
